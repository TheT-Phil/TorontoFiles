* CZYZ 2104 22/04/2021 *
************************
-Prop STARs/STARs with Common Transitions
	-Prop STARs are now selectable in the sector exit list. By default EuroScope will display the Jet STAR, but if you scroll and manually select the Prop STAR,
	 it will show up. Same goes for the TUKIR.IMEBA# and TUKIR.RAGID# arrivals, EuroScope will default to the IMEBA arrival as it comes first alphabetically,
	 but if you select the RAGID arrival manually it will show up properly in the sector exit list.
-Updated CJS/Primary Sectors
	-SH now primary owner of North/North Bay instead of YB
	-GR now primary owner of Low/West Low instead of SI/KF
	-WS now primary owner of Airports instead of XU
	-London Tower CJS changed from LT to XT
	-City Tower CJS changed from IT to TZ
-Groundlayout Updates
	-All airports updated with latest available satellite data
	-Added options for closures at YYZ/YTZ/YKF (Display settings > Regions > "Closures")
	-Deice pads can be toggled on/off as required (Display settings > Regions > "Deice Pads")
	-Added additional labels to all airports for terminals/buildings/deice/fbos (Display settings > Free Text)
	-Added deice transfer/hold points at YYZ (Display settings > Geo > "Ground Deice")
	-Added serviceroads to all groundlayouts
-Proper Toronto/Winnipeg Boundary
	-Updated aispace boundary to the correct/proper/real life one
	-Old FIR boundary can still be toggled (Display settings > ARTCC) for whatever ungodly reason you want to use that line
-Alternate Sector Ownership Profiles
	-Profiles to temporarily change the ownership of each sector.
	-When loaded this will only effect your scope (active/inactive sector backgrounds, active/inactive airports, etc.)
	-To enable temporarily, go to (Other settings > Sector ownership setup > Load "CZYZ AltOwnership") and press OK
		-MTL: Delegates Rupert, Noranda, Concordia and Ottawa to Montreal
		-DEP: Delegates East and West Satellite Sectors to Departure (or Arrival if Departure is offline)
		-OLD: Sets ownership to match with the AeroNav/GNG files
Miscellaneous Updates
	-Updated to AIRAC 2104
	-Added airports under the Sault Low extension & KCIU Control Zone
	-Updated YQG/YTR Positions
	-Updated Shoreline
	-Updated WPG sectors to reflect new positions and ownership
